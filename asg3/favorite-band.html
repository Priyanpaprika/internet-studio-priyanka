Assignment 4.5: Least Favorite Artist

Paramore

Short Band Bio

Paramore is an American rock band formed in Franklin, Tennessee, in 2004. Since 2017, the band's lineup includes lead vocalist Hayley Williams, lead guitarist Taylor York, and drummer Zac Farro.

Why I Love Them

This band made my middle school and high school years more better for sure. Hayley William's amazing voice really touched my heart, all throughout my emo/punk and alternative indie phases as well. They have has a lot of line-up changes, and things happen, but they still hold a very big place in my heart.
