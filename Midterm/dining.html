Places to Eat!


Wen Bakery

After people shop at Ba Sing Se for some quality merchandise, they always come for a sweet treat at Wen Bakery! Over here you can get an assortment of yummy desserts and pasteries such as Salted Duck Egg Pasteries. Yum!

Middle Ring Earthmarket

The Middle Ring of Ba Sing Se is known for being a cornacopia of wonderful sights and foods from all over the Earth Kingdom. Serving as the middle ground between the Upper Ring and the Lower Ring, it is guaranteed that everyone can find something that they are bound to enjoy. Come enjoy some bean curd puffs from Makapu Village, Braised turtle duck from the Upper Ring, moon peach cakes from Huijang, and more!

Cabbage Delicacies Bistro

You have defenitely seen his face around. The cabbage merchant is not only famous for the unfortunate demise of his cabbages but also for his yummy foods that he makes with them! He makes cabbage cookies, cabbage soup, cabbage noodles, and much more. Please help his business, he lowkey needs it after what the gang keeps doing to his produce...
